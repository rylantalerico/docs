With Crate.fm, you can store unlimited tracks. Here's a guide to upgrading your account:

## Crate.fm Pricing

If you're on the free tier of Crate.fm, you can store 10 songs forever. If you aren't familiar, here's a look at our pricing:

| Plan | Song limit       | GB limit |
| ---- | ---------------- | -------- |
| Free | 10 tracks        | 5GB      |
| Plus | 100 tracks       | 25GB     |
| Pro  | Unlimited tracks | 50GB     |

## Upgrading your Crate

<Info>This guide applies to the Crate.fm web and iOS app.</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    Navigate to your Crate.fm settings by clicking the menu icon in the top left
    corner of your Crate.fm app.
    <img className="block h-auto" src="/images/upgrade-1.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2">
    From your Crate.fm settings, click "Manage Plan."
    <img className="block h-auto" src="/images/upgrade-2.png" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3">
    On the "Manage Plan" screen, you can choose between Crate Plus and Crate
    Pro. On Crate.fm Plus, you can store and share up to 100 tracks, on Crate.fm
    Pro, you can unlimited tracks.
    <img className="block h-auto" src="/images/upgrade-3.png" />
  </Step>
</Steps>
