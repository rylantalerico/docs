## Upload your first track

We made uploading tracks to Crate.fm easy. Here's how:

### Uploading on iOS

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="apple" title="Upload from iMessage">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Step 1">
        Find the file you would like to upload from iMessage.
      </Step>

        <img className="block h-auto" src="/images/imessage-0.png" />

### Uploading on web

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion icon="arrow-pointer" title="Drag and drop">
  Drag and drop any audio file (or multiple files) into your Crate.fm library to
  import it.
  <img className="block h-auto" src="/images/dragndrop.png" />
</Accordion>
<Accordion icon="file" title="File selector">
  <Steps>
    <Step title="Step 1">
      Click the "Add" button in the top right corner of the screen.
      <img className="block h-auto" src="/images/desktop-import-1.png" />
    </Step>
    <Step title="Step 2">
      Next, click "Song."
      <img className="block h-auto" src="/images/desktop-import-2.png" />
    </Step>
    <Step title="Step 3">
      Crate.fm will open the file selector. Click the file (or files) you would like to upload and select "Open" in the bottom right corner of the file selector.
      <img className="block h-auto" src="/images/desktop-import-3.png" />
    </Step>
    <Step title="Step 4">
      That's it! Crate.fm will upload your track(s). While they're uploading, you can click "Edit" to give your song(s) a title, artist, and album artwork.
      <img className="block h-auto" src="/images/desktop-import-4.png" />
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</Accordion>
